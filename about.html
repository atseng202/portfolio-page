About

Full Stack Software Engineer

I am a full stack software engineer from Los Angeles, California with a B.S. degree in Physiological Sciences from University of California, Los Angeles. I have a background as a pharmacy technician and instructor for private and small classrooms.

My passion for software engineering first came about through curiosity about how to automate tasks at work, and later on a hobby in my spare time diving into the world of iOS development followed by the web.

Coming from health care, I was very empathetic to customer needs and have found this quality to be an important aspect of programming. From designing responsive features to making sure genuine needs are addressed, I find it exciting to discover ways to make thoughtful and life-changing products. I have also been teaching in small classrooms and for students privately from the ages of 10 to 19, and my time with education has been an eye-opener on setting up initial processes with kids, addressing their ongoing questions, and iterating on lessons until they were worthwhile for my students' time. I often asked what I could improve on to relay understanding, and educational tools were always available that I could test out. These only served to widen my curiosity of the endless tools that I could create with my own hands.

The beauty in this field for me is all the creative people working on lasting work for our world. From frictionless app experiences so our parents and grandparents can stay communicated with us, to tools engaging and educating communities on vital information, there are so many solutions being iterated on that will serve our world positively. I would love to connect sometime!
